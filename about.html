Hey there!

Have you always wanted to travel the world but don’t think you can afford it, or maybe you don’t have anyone to go with?

I used to feel the same way. I spent countless hours scrolling through Instagram, wondering how young people managed to travel so often. Then, I stumbled upon a travel vlog and learned how to make travel affordable and achievable. From there, my solo journey began.

In 2025, We created this blog to share adventures and inspire others to explore the world on a budget. Whether you’re a dreamer, a planner, or just someone seeking inspiration, this blog is for you.
